Slow Cooker Lamb Curry

★★★★★

Dinner, Pregnancy Super Food, Slow Cooker/Instant Pot

Prep Time: 10 min Cook Time: 6 hr Servings: Serves 2 easy Source: Bbcgoodfood.com

Ingredients

1 large onion halved and sliced

3 tbsp Madras curry paste

400g can chopped tomatoes

2 tsp vegetable bouillon powder

25g red lentils

210g can chickpeas (don’t drain)

Matchstick carrots

1 tbsp grated ginger

1 tsp turmeric

1 cinnamon stick

1 tsp red Chile flakes

75g curly kale

2 lean lamb steaks fat removed, diced (about 240g)


cooked brown rice to serve

Directions

Put all of the ingredients except kale into the slow cooker pot with a third of a can of water and stir well. Cover with the lid and chill in the fridge overnight.

The next day, stir again, then cook on Low for 6 hrs until the lamb and vegetables are tender. Add kale with 45 minutes remaining. Serve with brown rice.
