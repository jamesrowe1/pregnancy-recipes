Corn Salad Without Mayo

★★★★★

Pregnancy Super Food, Sides

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 15 mins Servings: Yields: 6 Source: Delish.com

Ingredients

2 cups fresh or frozen corn, rinsed and drained if frozen, and steamed, boiled, or grilled if fresh

or

1 (11 ounce) cans corn with poblano and red peppers, rinsed and drained (I use this one personally)

1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved or 1 diced roma tomato

1 avocado diced

1/4 red onion, finely chopped

Italian seasoning

3 Tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil

Juice of 1 lime

Kosher salt

Freshly ground black pepper

Directions


Combine all of your ingredients in the bowl, add your dressing, then toss everything to combine.

Toss all ingredients together in a large bowl, then season to taste with salt and pepper.
