Bibimbap

★★★★★

Dinner, Pregnancy Super Food, Slow Cooker/Instant Pot, Stovetop

Cook Time: 30 mins Servings: Servings: 4 Source: Carlsbadcravings.com

Ingredients

3 cups cooked brown rice

toasted sesame seeds for garnish

1 Flat Iron Steak or Flank steak (flank is a little leaner)

Asian BBQ Rub

Sesame oil for cooking

3 ounces dried shiitake mushrooms

1 english cucumber cut into batons

1 can sliced water chestnuts

1 teaspoon salt, divided

1 teaspoon pepper

1 TBS+½ tsp minced garlic, divided

1 teaspoon reduced sodium soy sauce

Gochujang Sauce

Directions

RICE:
Cook according to preference. A rice cooker makes this easy as you can set and forget.

BEEF:
Put rub on steak at least 30 minutes prior to grilling.
Preheat grill to medium for 10 minutes.
Cook, untouched on one side for at least 7 minutes.
Flip, cook for an additional 7ish minutes until steak reaches 125F.
Remove from heat, allow to rest for 10 minutes before slicing thinly against the grain.

VEGETABLES:
Chop and prepare vegetables
Heat sesame oil over medium-high heat.
Add mushrooms to oil, cook for a bit. Add carrots, water chestnuts, and cucumber, stir until covered in oil.
Season vegetables with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
Cook until cooked to taste.
Add gochujang paste, stir, and continue cooking for another minute.

FOR SERVING:
Place rice in bowl, top with beef, vegetables, and bean sprouts. Add soy sauce and extra gochujang as desired.
